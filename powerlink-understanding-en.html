1 What is Powerlink?

Core message: Powerlink is a keyword-based CPC ad that appears at the top of Naver search results.
ItemDetails
Official nameSite Search Ads (commonly known as: Powerlink, Keyword Search Ads)
PlacementTop of Naver integrated search results (PC + Mobile)
BillingCPC (Cost Per Click) — charged only when a click occurs
Domestic market share65% of search ad market
Nationwide reachReaches 71% of domestic internet users

Core Advantages of Powerlink

  • Ads appear at the exact moment a user with clear purchase intent performs a search
  • Exposure is free; only clicks cost money → budget-efficient
  • Runs on both Naver PC + Mobile simultaneously with no separate setup needed

2 Campaign Structure

4-Level Account Structure

Structure

  └── Campaign               ← Ad objective unit

Campaign

  • Role: Top-level advertising objective unit
  • Recommended: One business goal = one campaign
  • Settings: Campaign name, daily budget limit, scheduling

Ad Group

  • Role: Strategy unit — keywords + creatives sharing the same targeting and bid strategy
  • Settings: Device targeting (PC/mobile), region targeting, day/time targeting, base bid

Responsive Search Ads (RSA)

Core concept: Register multiple headlines and descriptions, and Naver AI automatically selects the best combination for display.
CategoryBasic CreativeResponsive Search Ad (RSA)
Headlines registered1Up to 15
Descriptions registered1Up to 4
Selection methodFixedAI auto-combination
CTR improvementBaseline+12% improvement

RSA Tips

  • Write 15 headlines from different angles (price-focused, benefit-focused, urgency-focused, etc.)
  • AI selects the best combination in real time based on the search query and user context
  • After performance data accumulates, continuously optimize by replacing low-performing headlines

5 Principles for Effective Ad Creatives

  1. Include keywords: naturally include the search term in the headline → appears bold, attracts attention
  2. Present clear benefits: use specific figures like "free shipping", "same-day dispatch", "30% off"
  3. Highlight differentiators: state unique strengths that set you apart from competitors
  4. Include a clear CTA: specific action prompts like "Check now", "Free consultation", "Buy now"
  5. Match the landing page: what you promise in the ad must actually exist on the landing page

Day/Time Targeting for Extensions

Ad extensions can be configured to display differently by day and time slot.

  • Weekday mornings → "Same-day shipping available" promotional text
  • Weekends → "Weekend special event" promotional text
  • During business hours only → Phone Number extension displayed

Short-tail Keywords

  • e.g., "sneakers", "insurance", "travel"
  • High search volume but includes users with unclear purchase intent
  • High CPC, budget drains quickly → use primarily for brand awareness

Long-tail Keywords

  • e.g., "Nike Air Force white size 270 men's", "diabetes insurance for men in their 30s"
  • Low search volume but users have clear purchase intent
  • Low CPC and high conversion rate → best ROI efficiency
  • Recommended first for beginners

Seasonal Keywords

  • e.g., "Valentine's Day gift ideas", "post-CSAT travel", "summer air conditioner recommendations"
  • Search volume spikes during specific seasons or events
  • Register 2–4 weeks before the season to allow quality stabilization before maximizing exposure

Keyword Research Methods

  • Naver Ad System keyword tool: check monthly search volume, competition level, and recommended bid
  • API: use the /keywordstool endpoint to collect large-scale keyword search volume data

5 ADVoost Search — AI-Powered Broad Match

Source: [Naver] ADVoost검색_플레이북_25년7월.pdf · As of April 2026: officially live

What is ADVoost Search?

Limitation of traditional Powerlink:
Ads only appear for keywords directly registered by the advertiser → if a searcher uses different terms, the ad is not shown
ADVoost Search innovation:
Naver AI analyzes the relevance between the search query and the landing page, and automatically shows ads for keywords the advertiser hasn't registered
Comparison 
[Traditional method]
Search: "Nike Air Max discount" → keyword must be registered → not shown if not registered

[ADVoost method]
Search: "Nike Air Max discount" → AI analyzes landing page
→ relevant content found on page → automatically shown

Why ADVoost Makes SEO Important

ADVoost's crawler reads the landing page and judges the relevance between the page topic and the search query.

  • Better SEO on landing page → AI shows ads for more related keywords
  • Poor SEO (text rendered as images, unnecessary redirects, etc.) → harder to benefit from ADVoost
  • For Powerlink advertisers, SEO is now essential, not optional
